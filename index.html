Sweet 16 Trip to Puerto Rico

When I turned 16 my father took me back to the place I was born, Puerto Rico!Before going
I was so excited because I had not been back since we moved away. I was only two when we moved
so all I knew about Puerto Rico came from what my family had told me through stories they remembered.
However, I knew that it was an island paradise that I would love too.

I have always loved the beach so what could be better than an island? When we got there the first
thing we did was go to our hotel. It was absolutely gorgeous and was shaped like a shell. The lobby
was decorated with beautiful furniture and was open to the outside so the cool ocean breeze was always flowing.

We stayed for a weekend, and while we were there we went on my adventures.
This website contains pictures and the highlights of our trip.

Vacation Activities

  • Went into The Butterfly People Store
  • Walked around Old San Juan
  • Visted Castillo San Felipe del Morro!
  • Went to the Beach
  • Went to a Party on the Street
  • Danced at Local Hotels
  • Listened to Bands on Street
